abstract: >-
  With the proliferation of a wide variety of sensors, accurate multi-source image registration is crucial for many remote sensing image processing tasks. However, the registration of multi-source images faces the challenges of rotations, scales, and domain transformations caused by significant differences in shooting time, viewing angle, and sensor imaging modes. To cope with this problem, we propose a deep learning-based registration method named TRFeat, which aims to comprehensively improve the rotation, scale, and cross-domain robustness of local features. First, we introduce a special circular sampling convolutional layer to replace the standard square convolutional layer, in order to enhance the rotational robustness of local features.
